Guide to Beating Nightmare King Grimm

In this guide, I will break down all of Grimm's moves / how to counter them as well as recommended charms

Grimm is one of the hardest bosses in Hollow Knight, so fighting him is no easy task, hopefully this guide will help make it more manageable

Recommended Charms

  1. Quick Focus
  2. Unbreakable Strength
  3. Shaman Stone
  4. Quick Slash
  5. Mark of Pride
  6. Longnail

Moveset

DASH SLASH

He dashes toward you while slashing.

You can avoid this by jumping over him and downslashing as he passes

FIRE PILLAR

Spawns four fire pillars underneath you.

You can avoid this by dashing horizontally as each pillar is about to hit you. In betweeen fire pillar spawns, he is very vulnerable, which allows you to get several hits.
